LinkHarbor Tutorial

  • Close the extension window and start clicking on the floating button to anchor websites!

  • Anchored sources will be saved in your folders, ready to be formatted and shared!

  • You can come back and organize your sources later.

  • For now, all sources will be saved in the same folder.

If you just enabled LinkHarbor, please refresh all your Chrome tabs first before proceeding!
